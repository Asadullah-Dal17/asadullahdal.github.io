About Me

Asadullah Dal

Computer Vision Engineer & AI Consultant

I am a Computer Vision Developer, freelancer, and teacher with a deep passion for artificial intelligence and technology. My journey in tech began with curiosity and has grown into a mission to build robust, real-world AI solutions that make a difference.

Over the past decade, I have worked on a diverse range of projects, from developing advanced computer vision systems for industry to creating educational content that empowers others to learn and innovate. My work has spanned domains such as healthcare, robotics, education, and automation, where I have delivered solutions that are both innovative and practical.

I am a strong advocate for open knowledge and believe in the power of community-driven learning. Through my YouTube channel, blog, and workshops, I share insights, tutorials, and project walkthroughs to help others master computer vision and AI. My teaching philosophy centers on making complex topics accessible and inspiring curiosity in learners of all backgrounds.

My technical expertise includes AI-driven product development, machine learning, deep learning, and deploying scalable solutions. I enjoy collaborating with diverse teams, mentoring aspiring developers, and helping organizations turn ideas into impactful, AI-powered products. My approach combines technical rigor with creativity and a focus on real-world impact.

  • Current: Junior School Teacher at Government Boys' High School Dadoon, where I inspire young minds in STEM and foster a love for technology and problem-solving. I am dedicated to nurturing the next generation of innovators and critical thinkers.
  • 2025 Goal: Launch more free courses on YouTube, including a comprehensive Mediapipe Course to make advanced computer vision accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or resources.

I am always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and meaningful conversations. Whether you are looking to build the next big thing in AI, need guidance on your learning journey, or want to connect over shared interests, feel free to reach out. Together, we can turn ideas into reality and shape the future of technology.
